Website for Lips&Lips

Web Development/Visual Designs

Brand Ideas

We begin this project with the brand ideas. We created the brand of an online store called Lips&Lips which sells lipsticks import from Asia. As the brand from Asia is very popular in the young groups, our target audience are teenager girls who like brand of lipstick from Asia with fashion design.

Color and Fonts

To makes the website for our targeted audiences, we used pink as background as pink is the color contains the meaning of beauty and cute. Online shopping is a relaxing thing to do, we used light pink for background to create a feeling of chill and relax.

To make the contrast, we applied black and large font size for easy read and use grey color or small font size to prevent distraction for the part which is less important, such as navigation links.

Design

As an online shopper myself, the things annoyed me the most of time are the complex contents and squeezed texts. In this project, I put enough space between different elements and try to use as less decoration as possible.

For the payment form, I used lines instead of the square to makes the contents clear and create a minimal look.
