Website for Teenager Suicide Prevention Education

Web Development/Graphic Design

Topic Research

In this project, me and my teammates decide to make a website to raise the attentions on teenager’s suicides as it is the second main cause for their death in Canada. During the research, we collected the data, evidences and related information.

Color and Fonts

Since the three key words for this topic are “Death”, “Warnings” and “Danger”, we decided to use red and black as primary colors. For the font, we used sans-serif because of our contents are more related to the modern problems. Red color applied to main title.

To create the contrast, we added yellow as it is a color have the meaning of warning. Moreover, we added blue color as a contrast to red and yellow. Yellow is for the subtitles and blue for the descriptions.

Design

Combining with the text, we designed some graphs and icons to makes our audiences to easier understand the contents or visualize the data as a diagram for clear comparison.

The icons we designed indicates different suicide methods and when user hover their mouse over the icon, the related contents will pop up for detail explanation.

We applied the pie chart visualize the percentage distribution of the suicide methods based on gender. Using pie chart can clearly indicates which method distribute the most or least in total.
